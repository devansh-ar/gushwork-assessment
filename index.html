Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of a laser cutter for sheet metal?

It is designed to cut various types of sheet metal with precision using laser technology. HDPE pipes benefit from laser-cut fittings that ensure perfect alignment and leak-free connections in complex piping networks.

What materials can HDPE pipes transport safely?

HDPE pipes can safely transport potable water, natural gas, chemicals, slurries, and sewage. Their chemical resistance makes them suitable for a wide range of industrial and municipal applications.

What is the expected lifespan of HDPE piping systems?

HDPE pipes have a proven service life of 50+ years under normal operating conditions. With proper installation and maintenance, they can exceed this lifespan significantly.
